About ADNU MAGIS TBI.

ADNU MAGIS TBI is a university-owned and operated technology business incubator serving technology-focused or technology-utilizing growth-oriented startups. With development and scale in mind, it aims to provide access to market and resources, allowing startups to propel into success.

OUR VISION-

MAGIS TBI aspires to be the Bicol region's innovation hub, empowering local tech entrepreneurs through collaboration, mentorship, and Ignatian values to drive economic growth and social progress. They aim to nurture graduates not just as successful entrepreneurs, but also as responsible citizens committed to serving their communities.

OUR MISSION-

MAGIS TBI empowers aspiring tech entrepreneurs with resources and mentorship, fostering not just successful businesses, but also individuals driven to serve and excel for the greater good. In essence, we build "men and women for others" in the tech world.

OUR GOALS-

Support and develop ideas and research to become impactful, sustainable, and innovative startups

Empower and support current and aspiring entrepreneurs and MSMEs with our programs and services

Deliver Impact and provide opportunities to the Bicol Region.

We are a DOST-PCIEERD Funded TBI under the HEIRIT Program
